Unlike Ozick, Puttermesser lives in a magical realist version of New York, one in which job disappointments can lead to the accidental creation of golems which then help one become Mayor of New York City. Tragic and comic by turns, //The Puttermesser Papers// is a good a start as any to Ozick's work.
